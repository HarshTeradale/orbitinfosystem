About Us

ORBIT INFO SYSTEMS is a medium-sized company providing comprehensive IT and electronic security solutions including CCTV installation, integration, and maintenance services. Our team specializes in biometric systems, networking infrastructure, and comprehensive IT support.

Established in 2012, Orbit has grown into a trusted partner for businesses seeking reliable technology solutions. With deep expertise across CCTV systems, networking, biometric security, computer hardware, and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC), we deliver integrated solutions that enhance security and operational efficiency. Our commitment to excellence, coupled with 24/7 customer support, ensures your systems run seamlessly.
